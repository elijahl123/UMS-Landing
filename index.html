Clean, Efficient Dashboard

Clearly visualize your workload

With the UMS Dashboard, you will be able to view upcoming assignments and events, late assignments, and your next course for the day.

Integrated Calendar

Organize your month 

With the Calendar feature, all your assignments, upcoming events, and courses will appear automatically on the calendar when you upload them

Course Schedule

Map out your courses for the week

With the schedule feature, you will be able to see your classes for the week along with their location on campus. You can even link directly to Zoom if the class is online!

Keep Track of Notes

Take better notes, faster

UMS lets you create notes, which can include text, images, audio, and web clippings. You can organize your notes by course, and access them from any device.

Organize Your Assignments

Never miss a due date

With the homework feature, you can be reminded of upcoming assignments and even get daily alerts to help progress on reading assignments.
